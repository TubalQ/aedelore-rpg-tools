# Ashendor — Complete World Geography

## Detailed Location List (Reference)

### REGIONS
| # | Name | Position | Description |
|---|------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | Thorsheim | Entire northern edge, west→east | Colossal snow-covered mountain range. Dragon Gods sleep here. Impassable barrier. Arctic. |
| 2 | Brightwoods | Northwest, under Thorsheim | Vast dark corrupted forest. Dangerous, untamed. Sunken City at southern end. Cold temperate. |
| 3 | Mount Basin | North-central, under Great Tree | Mountainous area forming the border between Westfall and Eastwatch. |
| 4 | Borea | Northeast, under Thorsheim | Frozen mountains, ice and snow year-round. Dwarven homeland. Large waterfall on eastern side. Arctic/alpine. |
| 5 | Westfall | Western half, south of Brightwoods | Farmland, forests, western coast. Generally peaceful. Temperate. |
| 6 | Eastwatch | Eastern half, south of Borea | Forests, farmland, eastern coast. Bay with islands to the south. Temperate. |
| 7 | Northern Marches | Border zone between Westfall and Eastwatch | Transitional area. Mixed landscape. Temperate. |
| 8 | Alfwyld Forest | Northern Eastwatch, toward southern Mount Basin | Ancient forest. Halfling homeland. Lush, warm temperate. |
| 9 | The Ivory Bogs | Southwestern Arfalia, near coast | Bog/wetland. Feldale and Varrow located here. |
| 10 | Arfalia | Southern continent, south of Northern Marches | Mix of mountain ranges, temperate and arid. Mediterranean in south. |
| 11 | The Burning Passage | Southwest | Volcanic, orange/red terrain. Hot, dry, desolate. Uninhabited. |
| 12 | Orcish Territory | Islands east of Fort Salinax | Orc and troll domains beyond the sea. |
