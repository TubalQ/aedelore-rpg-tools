2. Combat Rules

Initiative

  • 6 or fewer participants: Roll 1D6 (1 = slowest, 6 = fastest)
  • More than 6 participants: Roll 1D10 instead

Attack

Attack Pool = Core Ability + Weapon Attack Bonus
(Convert each part to dice, max 8D10 total)
SuccessesResult
0Miss - no damage
1Glancing blow - 50% damage
2+Full hit - full damage
Critical (10)Extra effect (DM decides)

Defense Options

Dodge

Pool = Dexterity + Acrobatics
  • Success: Avoid attack completely
  • Barely: Take half damage
  • Fail: Take full damage

Parry

Pool = Strength + Weapon Attack Bonus
  • Success: Parry attack, no damage
  • Barely: Reduce damage by weapon's attack bonus
  • Fail: Take full damage

Block (requires shield or armor)

Pool = Strength
  • Success (at least 1): Block up to shield's/armor's Block value. Remaining damage goes through. Shield takes blocked damage to its HP.
  • Fail: Take full damage

Take Hit

Pool = Toughness + Armor Bonus

Each success reduces damage by 1.

5. HP Calculation

Total HP = Race Base HP + Class HP Bonus

Examples:

  • Human (20) + Warrior (+5) = 25 HP
  • Orc (24) + Warrior (+5) = 29 HP
  • Halfling (14) + Mage (+2) = 16 HP
  • Dwarf (22) + Outcast (+4) = 26 HP

6. Worthiness Calculation

Total Worthiness = Race Worthiness + Class Worthiness + Religion Modifier

Examples:

  • Human (7) + Warrior (+3) = 10 Worthiness
  • Orc (-5) + Warrior (+3) = -2 Worthiness
  • High Elf (10) + Thief (-4) = 6 Worthiness
  • Human (7) + Mage (+6) + Abyssal Veil (-5) = 8 Worthiness

11. Status Effects

Weakened (Exhaustion)

  • Each character has 6 Weakened Points
  • Using abilities costs Weakened
  • When Weakened = 0: Roll 1D10 + Toughness
    • 1-2: Death
    • 3-5: Unconscious
    • 6-10: Exhausted (can move but not fight)
  • Recovery: Short rest = +1 Weakened, Long rest = all restored

Bleed (Bleeding)

  • When you receive a critical wound, you start bleeding
  • Each round: Roll 1D6 (1-2 = 3 HP, 3-4 = 2 HP, 5-6 = 1 HP loss)
  • Bleed [X]: Roll X dice, take highest
  • Stop bleeding: Medicine check (1 success per Bleed level), Bandage (Bleed 1), Healing magic (all)

Other Effects

12. Healing & Rest

Natural Healing

  • Short rest (1 hour): +1D6 HP, +1 Weakened
  • Long rest (8 hours): All HP restored, all Weakened restored

Medicine Check

  • 1 success: +1D6 HP to patient
  • 2+ successes: +2D6 HP to patient
  • Critical: +3D6 HP to patient

13. Resources

Food & Water

  • Tracked with dice (D10 → D6 → out)
  • Daily: Roll your food die. 1-5 = reduce die size
  • Without: 1 day = no effect, 2 days = -1D10 all rolls, 3+ days = 1 dmg/day

Ammunition

  • After combat: Roll 1D6 to recover (1-2 = 25%, 3-4 = 50%, 5-6 = 75%)
  • Cost: Arrows/Bolts (20-50) = 1 gold, Sling Bullets (20) = 4 copper

Arcane Elixir

  • Starting: Mage = 2, Druid = 1, Others = 0
  • Max carried: 4
  • Effect: Restores 10 Arcana

14. Character Creation Summary

  1. Choose Race (gives base HP, Worthiness, bonuses)
  2. Choose Class (gives HP bonus, equipment, abilities)
  3. Choose Religion (optional, gives bonuses)
  4. Distribute 10 extra points among talents (max 5 per talent at start)
  5. Note starting equipment from Race + Class
  6. Calculate Total HP = Race HP + Class HP Bonus
  7. Calculate Total Worthiness = Race + Class + Religion

15. Quick Reference

Attack Sequence

1. Build Attack Pool = Core Ability + Weapon Attack Bonus (convert to dice)
2. Roll pool (max 8D10)
3. Count successes (7-9 = success, 10 = critical + reroll)
4. Determine hit: 0 = miss, 1 = 50% dmg, 2+ = full dmg
5. Roll weapon damage dice

Defense Sequence

1. Choose defense type (Dodge, Parry, Block, Take Hit)
2. Build Defense Pool (varies by type)
3. Roll pool
4. Count successes
5. Apply result (success = no damage, barely = partial, fail = full)

Spell Casting (Mage/Druid)

1. Choose spell
2. Check Arcana cost
3. Roll Arcana check (need at least 1 success)
4. On success: Pay Arcana cost, apply spell effect
5. On fail: DM decides (fizzle or backlash)

Ability Usage (Melee Classes)

1. Check ability's Check requirement
2. Roll the check (e.g., "Stealth, min 2 success")
3. On success: Pay Weakened cost
4. Gain the bonus dice for your action
